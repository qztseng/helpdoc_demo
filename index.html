What is osteoporosis?什麼是骨質疏鬆症？

Osteoporosis is a condition where bones become weak and brittle, greatly increasing fracture risk. It is often called a "silent disease" because bone loss has no symptoms until a fracture occurs. In Taiwan, approximately one in three women and one in five men over the age of 50 will experience an osteoporotic fracture. Hip fractures are particularly serious — up to 20% of patients die within the first year, and many lose their independence.

骨質疏鬆症是骨骼變得脆弱、容易骨折的疾病，常被稱為「沉默的疾病」，因為骨質流失通常沒有症狀，直到發生骨折才被發現。在台灣，50 歲以上的族群中，約每 3 位女性就有 1 位、每 5 位男性就有 1 位會發生骨質疏鬆性骨折。其中髖部骨折尤為嚴重 — 約 20% 的患者在一年內死亡，許多人因此失去生活自理能力。

The good news: osteoporosis can be prevented and treated 好消息：骨質疏鬆症是可以預防與治療的 Early detection through bone density screening allows timely intervention — before a fracture occurs. This report is one such screening result. 透過骨密度篩檢及早發現，可以在骨折發生前及時介入治療。這份報告就是一項篩檢結果。

T-Score & Z-Score in Depth

T 值與 Z 值深入說明

T-Score

T 值

Your T-score tells you how your BMD compares to the average BMD of a young healthy adult at peak bone mass, expressed in standard deviations (SD). For example, T = −1.5 means your BMD is 1.5 SD below the young adult mean.

T 值告訴您，您的 BMD 與年輕健康成人在骨量高峰時的平均值相差幾個標準差 (SD)。例如 T = −1.5 表示您的 BMD 低於年輕成人平均值 1.5 個標準差。

The WHO classification (Normal / Osteopenia / Osteoporosis) is applicable to postmenopausal women and men aged 50 and older, per ISCD and TOA guidelines.

WHO 分類（正常/骨質缺乏（低骨量）/骨質疏鬆症）依 ISCD 與 TOA 指引，適用於停經後女性及 50 歲以上男性。

Z-Score

Z 值

Your Z-score compares your BMD to people of the same age, sex, and ethnic background. It is more relevant for premenopausal women and men under 50. A Z-score ≤ −2.0 is classified as "below the expected range for age".

Z 值將您的 BMD 與同年齡、同性別人群比較。對停經前女性與 50 歲以下男性更具參考意義。Z 值 ≤ −2.0 被歸類為「低於年齡預期範圍」。

Reference population for T-score T 值的參考族群 As recommended by the ISCD and TOA, DeepXray uses the NHANES III young (20–29) non-Hispanic white female reference population for T-score calculation. This standardised reference is used for all patients regardless of sex or ethnicity. 依據 ISCD 與 TOA 建議，DeepXray 使用 NHANES III 年輕（20–29 歲）非西班牙裔白人女性作為 T 值計算的參考族群。此標準化的參考族群適用於所有患者，不分性別或種族。

How the average BMD is calculated

BMD 均值如何計算

The summary row shows the area-weighted average of the selected vertebrae — not simply the arithmetic mean of four values. This means larger vertebrae contribute proportionally more, consistent with how DXA machines calculate the spine summary.

平均值列顯示的是所選椎體的面積加權平均值 — 並非四個數值的簡單算術平均。較大的椎體在計算中貢獻更大比例，與 DXA 儀器計算脊椎均值的方式一致。

Classification uses the average, not the lowest individual vertebra 分類使用均值，而非最低的個別椎體 Per ISCD guidelines, the spine WHO classification is determined by the L1–L4 average T-score, not by the worst (lowest) T-score of any single vertebra. Using the lowest single vertebra would overestimate fracture risk. 依據 ISCD 指引，脊椎的 WHO 分類由 L1–L4 平均 T 值決定，而非任何單一椎體最差（最低）的 T 值。使用最低的單一椎體會高估骨折風險。

When vertebrae are excluded

當椎體被排除時

If a vertebra has a fracture, implant, or structural abnormality, your doctor may exclude it. The average is recalculated from the remaining vertebrae (e.g. "L134" if L2 is excluded). Per ISCD guidance, at least two vertebrae must remain. If only one is valid, no average or WHO classification will be shown.

若某椎體有骨折、植入物或結構異常，醫師可能會將其排除。均值會使用剩餘椎體重新計算（如 L2 被排除則顯示「L134」）。依 ISCD 指引，至少須保留兩個椎體。若僅剩一個有效椎體，將不會顯示均值或 WHO 分類。

Since DeepXray's AI model is trained against DXA measurements, these characteristics carry over. That said, Total Hip is also not immune to variability — for example, the length of femoral shaft included in the analysis region can vary with imaging positioning. 股骨頸的感興趣區域 (ROI) 較小。在 DXA 中，ROI 的精確放置會因操作者的擺位而產生差異，導致量測變異性較大。由於 DeepXray 的 AI 模型是根據 DXA 量測數據訓練的，這些特性也會延續。但全髖部也非完全不受影響 — 例如，納入分析區域的股骨幹長度可能因影像擺位不同而改變。

參考族群差異

In Taiwan, different hospitals may still use different reference populations to calculate T-scores, despite the ISCD/TOA recommendation to use the NHANES III young white female reference. If a facility uses a different reference database, the resulting T-scores can differ significantly even when the underlying BMD values are similar. DeepXray follows the ISCD/TOA recommendation.

在台灣，儘管 ISCD/TOA 建議使用 NHANES III 年輕白人女性參考族群，不同醫院可能仍使用不同的參考族群來計算 T 值。若醫療機構使用不同的參考資料庫，即使 BMD 值相似，所得的 T 值也可能有顯著差異。DeepXray 遵循 ISCD/TOA 建議。

DXA vendor differences & cross-calibration

DXA 廠牌差異與交叉校正

DeepXray's AI model was trained and validated against GE DXA BMD measurements. Different DXA vendors (GE, Hologic, etc.) may report slightly different absolute BMD values for the same patient due to differences in hardware, software, and calibration. Published cross-calibration conversion formulae exist (from DXA manufacturers and international research teams) that can compensate for the systematic bias between vendors. However, even after conversion, random error between individual measurements persists, and results should not be considered interchangeable at the individual patient level.

DeepXray 的 AI 模型是根據 GE DXA 的 BMD 量測數據進行訓練與驗證的。不同的 DXA 廠牌（GE、Hologic 等）由於硬體、軟體及校正方式的差異，可能會對同一位患者報告略微不同的 BMD 絕對值。DXA 廠商及國際研究團隊已發表交叉校正轉換公式，可以補償廠牌間的系統性偏差。然而，即使經過轉換，個別量測之間的隨機誤差仍然存在，結果在個別患者層面上不應被視為完全可互換。

Practical advice 實用建議 For monitoring bone density changes over time, it is best to use the same device, same technology, and same facility whenever possible. If you switch devices or facilities, inform your doctor so they can account for potential measurement differences. 追蹤骨密度隨時間的變化時，最好盡可能使用相同的設備、相同的技術及相同的醫療機構。若您更換設備或機構，請告知您的醫師，以便他們考量潛在的量測差異。

What Is Bone Mineral Density (BMD)?

什麼是骨密度 (BMD)？

Bone Mineral Density (BMD) measures how much mineral content — primarily calcium and phosphorus — is present in a projected area of bone. It is reported as areal BMD in units of g/cm² (grams per square centimetre). This is an areal density, not a volumetric density (g/cm³ in everyday physics), because both DXA and X-ray-based methods measure bone from a two-dimensional X-ray projection. DeepXray uses the same areal BMD unit (g/cm²) as DXA, estimated using the radiogrammetry principle applied to the 2D X-ray image.

骨密度（BMD）衡量骨骼投影面積中的礦物質含量（主要為鈣與磷），單位為 g/cm²（克/平方公分）。這是面積密度（areal BMD），與物理學上的體積密度（g/cm³）不同。DXA 基於二維 X 光投影原理，所測得的也是面積密度。DeepXray 與 DXA 採用相同的面積密度單位（g/cm²），是透過應用於二維 X 光影像的放射計量（radiogrammetry）原理來推測 BMD。

A higher BMD generally means stronger, denser bones. As people age, bones naturally lose minerals and become less dense, increasing fracture risk. BMD is one of the strongest single predictors of fracture risk, which is why regular screening is important.

BMD 值越高通常代表骨骼越強壯、越緻密。隨著年齡增長，骨骼礦物質自然流失、密度降低，骨折風險隨之增加。BMD 是預測骨折風險最重要的單一指標之一，因此定期篩檢非常重要。

Frequently Asked Questions

常見問題

My T-score is Osteopenia. Does that mean I will get osteoporosis?我的 T 值是骨質缺乏（低骨量），代表我會得骨質疏鬆症嗎？

Not necessarily. Osteopenia means your bone density is lower than the young adult average but not yet osteoporosis. With appropriate measures — calcium, vitamin D, exercise, and lifestyle changes — many people can slow or reverse bone loss. Talk to your doctor.

不一定。骨質缺乏（低骨量）代表您的骨密度低於年輕成人平均值，但尚未達骨質疏鬆症。透過適當措施 — 鈣質、維生素 D、運動及生活型態調整 — 許多人可以減緩甚至逆轉骨質流失。請與醫師討論。

Why does each vertebra show a different T-score?為什麼每個椎體的 T 值都不同？

Each vertebra has a different natural bone density and a different site-specific reference value. A vertebra with a lower BMD may not have the lowest T-score because it is compared against a different (possibly lower) reference mean. The overall average is what matters.

每個椎體有不同的自然骨密度與不同的部位特異性參考值。BMD 較低的椎體其 T 值不一定最低，因為比較對象是不同的（可能較低的）參考平均值。重要的是整體均值。

Why was a vertebra excluded from my report?為什麼某個椎體被排除？

If a vertebra has a fracture, implant, or abnormality that would make its measurement unreliable, it may be excluded. This follows ISCD standard practice. At least two vertebrae must remain for a valid average.

若某椎體有骨折、植入物或異常使其量測不可靠，可能被排除。這符合 ISCD 標準做法。至少須保留兩個椎體才能計算有效均值。

Spine says "Normal" but hip says "Osteopenia" — which counts?脊椎「正常」但髖部「骨質缺乏」— 以哪個為準？

The overall classification uses the site with the lowest T-score. If your hip shows Osteopenia and spine shows Normal, the overall result is Osteopenia. Different body sites commonly show different bone density levels.

整體分類取 T 值最低的部位。若髖部為骨質缺乏而脊椎正常，整體結果為骨質缺乏。不同身體部位顯示不同程度的骨密度是很常見的。

I am under 50. Are the results valid?我未滿 50 歲，結果有效嗎？

DeepXray is cleared by TFDA for adult women (age 20 and above) and men age 40 and above. For men under 40, osteoporosis prevalence is extremely low in this population and current data lack sufficient evidence to support reliable performance — the results may not be applicable. For premenopausal women and men under 50, interpretation differs from older adults: the Z-score (comparison with peers of the same age) is preferred over the T-score, as recommended by ISCD and TOA. The WHO T-score categories generally should not be applied to these groups. Discuss your results with your doctor.

DeepXray 已獲 TFDA 核准，適用對象為成年女性（20 歲以上）及 40 歲以上男性。對於未滿 40 歲的男性，此族群骨質疏鬆症盛行率極低，目前資料尚缺乏足夠證據支持本工具在此族群的合理表現，結果可能不適用。對停經前女性與 50 歲以下男性，解讀方式與年長族群不同：依據 ISCD 及 TOA 建議，應優先使用 Z 值（與同齡族群比較）而非 T 值，WHO 的 T 值分類通常不應套用於這些族群。請與醫師討論您的檢測結果。

My previous DXA T-score is different from this report. Why?我之前 DXA 的 T 值與這份報告不同，為什麼？

There are several possible reasons: different reference populations, different DXA vendors (GE vs. Hologic), different measurement technology (DXA vs. AI-based estimation), actual bone density changes over time, or normal measurement variability. Direct comparison of T-scores across different devices and technologies should be done with caution. Consult your doctor for proper interpretation.

可能原因包括：不同的參考族群、不同的 DXA 廠牌（GE vs. Hologic）、不同的量測技術（DXA vs. AI 估測）、實際骨密度隨時間變化，或正常的量測變異。不同設備與技術之間的 T 值直接比較應謹慎。請諮詢醫師以獲得正確解讀。

What can I do to protect my bones?我可以做什麼來保護骨骼？

Ensure adequate daily calcium (1,000–1,200 mg) and vitamin D; engage in regular weight-bearing and muscle-strengthening exercises; avoid smoking and limit alcohol; take fall-prevention measures if older. If at elevated risk, medications are available. Consult your healthcare provider.

確保每日攝取足夠鈣質（1,000–1,200 毫克）與維生素 D；規律進行負重與肌力訓練運動；避免吸菸並限制飲酒；年長者應採取防跌措施。若風險較高，有藥物選項可用。請諮詢醫療照護提供者。
